Our Alumni

Here at Wits Digital Arts, our students are our priority. Our Alumni are the result of our holistic outlook on education, preparation and skill-development. They have joined us at different stages, from different paths of life, and with different goals, passions and mindsets. Here, we honor and celebrate a few of them and their achievements in their respective industries - and showcase to you, just what diverse paths you can achieve with the help of Wits Digital Arts, as you pursue your passions and create your goals.

Our Students

Digital Arts produces graduates with a deep understanding of the theories and practice of Technology Arts. Our focus is on producing students that demonstrate adaptability and critical understanding in professional arenas: as independent artists, entrepreneurs, as well as in the more formalized workforce.
