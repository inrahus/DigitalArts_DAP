Our Digital Homecoming!

Every year, the Digital Arts Department hosts an exhibition in honour of our soon-to-be graduates, who can showcase their long term work and interact with local industry professionals!

This year, the theme of our exhibition, is "Digital Homecoming". This symbolises both the move into our own on-campus facilities, and a chance for our students to touch base with us after a long battle with the pandemic and the turmoil it has presented them.

The above poster was design by one of our very own students, and captures the nature of this year's theme to a tee!

With the onset of the new omnicron variant, we are doing everything possible to make sure outr students get the exhibition they deserve. With h=this being said, we have decided to postpone our event.
