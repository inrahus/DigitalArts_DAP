About Us

What We Do

At the Digital Arts Department, we focus on producing graduates with a deep understanding of the theories and practice of Technology Arts.

Our three key areas of interest are: Animation, Game Design and Interactive Media. Our students leave with diverse skill sets: technically, theoretically, and artistically. These elements are addressed holistically throughout our programs. Our focus is on producing students that demonstrate adaptability and critical understanding in professional arenas: as independent artists, entrepreneurs, as well as in the more formalized workforce.

Digital technologies are driving factors in economic and cultural development throughout Africa, and for our graduates to enter this space they have to be both technically and critically equipped to work within this challenging environment.
